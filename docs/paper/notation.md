# Notation {.unnumbered}

## Entity Identifiers {.unnumbered .unlisted}

Each entity is numbered sequentially starting from 1.
Unique identifiers consist of two capital letters indicating the object type, followed by a number indicating the specific instance.

Jurisdictions:

- Nation: NL0000
- Region (Landsdeel): LD0000
- Province: PV0000
- Municipality (Gemeente): GM0000

Water Sources:

- Groundwater: SG0000
- Surface water: SS0000
- Desalination: SD0000

Infrastructure:

- Pumping stations: PS0000 (uniquely determines the source)
- Pump option: PU000

Connections:

- Between municipalities: CG0000
- From source to municipality: CS0000
- Between provinces: CP0000
- Pipe option: PI000
